🌄 Things to do nearby
🏰Villages & Culture
🗼
Radicofani
Medieval village with the fortress of Ghino di Tacco and the church of Sant'Agata. Stunning views over the Val d'Orcia.
🏛️
Pienza
UNESCO World Heritage site, the "ideal Renaissance city". Famous for its DOP pecorino cheese. 30 min away.
🍷
Montalcino
Home of Brunello, one of Italy's finest wines. Medieval fortress and wineries in the historic centre. 40 min away.
⛪
Sant'Antimo Abbey
Beautiful Romanesque abbey near Montalcino, surrounded by olive trees. Monks still sing Gregorian chant. 45 min away.
🎨
Montepulciano
Renaissance hilltop town famous for Vino Nobile. Piazza Grande is one of the most beautiful in Tuscany. 30 min away.
🌿
Bagno Vignoni
Unique village with a large Renaissance thermal pool at its centre. 20 min away.
♨️Thermal Baths & Wellness
💧
Bagno Vignoni Thermal Baths
Natural thermal pools with sulphurous water at 52°C. Adler Spa and Posta Marcucci offer access. 20 min away.
🌊
San Filippo Thermal Springs
Free natural white thermal waterfalls in the forest. One of the most spectacular in Tuscany. 25 min away.
🏊
Chianciano Terme
Renowned thermal resort with water parks and wellness centres. Great for families. 25 min away.
🥾Nature & Hiking
🌾
Val d'Orcia
UNESCO World Heritage landscape. Rolling hills with cypress trees and clay hills among the most photographed in the world.
🌋
Monte Amiata
Tuscany's highest extinct volcano (1738 m). Forest trails, mountain biking and skiing in winter. 20 min away.
🚶
Via Francigena
The ancient pilgrims' route passes through Radicofani. Sections walkable on foot through stunning scenery.
🦅
Lucciola Bella Nature Reserve
Natural oasis with clay hills and typical Val d'Orcia flora. Great for birdwatching. Free entry.
